[ ​Spear of the Precipice​ ]

A man attempts to climb a massive, unconquered mountain. It's as if he searches for the boundary between life and death through the harshness of nature and finds purpose in it.
───────────────
Suddenly, a blizzard sets upon him. He feels for the gift from his daughter in his pocket and continues to climb, letting the warmth from her present carry him.
───────────────
When he finally reaches the summit, he beholds an illusion of the wife he left behind. He then swears to make it down the mountain and return to the warmth of his happy family.
───────────────
Only the faintest hint of life remains in the man after he slips down the cliff. The image of his daughter and his pregnant wife scatter into darkness. Is it regret? Or a brief moment of joy?

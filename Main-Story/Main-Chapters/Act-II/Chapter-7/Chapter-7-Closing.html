- Weapon Type: 2H Sword -

A man who lives with his wife and son in a futuristic world. One day, his happy, uneventful existence is shattered by the sudden appearance of flowering monstrosities that claim the life of his child.

After that, his feelings of vengeance towards the Flowers became his sole reason for living.

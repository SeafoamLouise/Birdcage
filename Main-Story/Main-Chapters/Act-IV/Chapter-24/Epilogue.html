Below this are some additional pieces of artwork for the game and the final "Life in The Cage" comic strip.

Artwork by Momojiru

Momojiru is the artist for all of the "Life in The Cage" comics. Note that Sarafa was in the original sketch for this artwork, but was accidentally left out in the final version.

Artwork by D.K

D.K was one of the character designers for NieR Gestalt/RepliCant.

Artwork by Koda Kazuma

Koda Kazuma does a large amount of artwork for the series, such as album art and other promotional material.

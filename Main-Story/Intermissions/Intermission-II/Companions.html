Companions

This is a small little game mechanic that exists that wasn't touched on before; Companions​! This was mentioned in one of the earlier chapters by Mama to Levania when she asks on if he would like a plush toy or something else.

These would act as a one-time use skill per fight, and each party member of your team could have one Companion (for a total of three different Companions). You could upgrade these for greater effect on their strength.

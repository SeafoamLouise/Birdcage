Final Gameplay Bits

Before the third arc, let's wrap up any final details of gameplay stuff that has been neglected so far. A quick-fire round! There are still more things outside what this page describes, but we'll make things fast!

Back again to “Mama’s Room,” the main hub of the game which we talked about at the very start of the game, is something small. You could click on those little glowing white orbs in the center (they fall from the tree above sometimes) and they will give you a few free items and goods. Thank you Mama!

You could get a log in bonus each day for logging in. Some items included were Stamina Potions! Yes, the game had stamina, but no, it wasn't a problem. You got so many potions that you would never really have lacking stamina; it was easy to have hundreds upon hundreds in reserve.

You also had a cool user profile! I forgot to take a picture of mine before the EoS sadly, but kindly enough have been supplied this one from PigmaskGuy. Your profile would chart a lot of your data and information for you and you could display three characters.

You also had a little gift box that stored free stuff you would sometimes be sent for playing the game or excess/overflow items (for once you clear some more inventory) or from doing a few secret missions (ex. tap Mama 100 times, clear certain stages).

There was the special gift roulette, a wheel you could spin in hopes of getting 3000 gems which are equal to a whole ten pulls. The chances of getting this was so low that barely anybody ever did...

If you got lucky, the door for summons would sometimes change to be a Mama door; you know your pulls are going to be good seeing this! And Carrier will cameo every now and then as well!

If you enabled notifications, sometimes Mama will send you a little message notification. She still could do this after the end of service, so it's a little heartbreaking to see sometimes knowing the game is gone... That's also what the game icon looked like on the left if you were curious.

There was a photo and pose mode in the game so that you could get cool pictures of your characters doing different things! You could up to a full team of three characters for this and have fun making images of your party! Above are a few taken by u/budgieboi7 on Reddit.
